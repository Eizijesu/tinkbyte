# Cookie Policy

**Effective Date:** January 15, 2025

## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better browsing experience by remembering your preferences and understanding how you use our site.

## How We Use Cookies

**Essential Cookies:** These cookies are necessary for our website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as:

- Security features and user authentication
- Session management and navigation
- Basic website functionality
- Remembering your privacy preferences

**Analytics Cookies:** We use these cookies to understand how visitors interact with our website:

- **Google Analytics:** Helps us analyze website traffic and user behavior
- **Performance monitoring:** Tracks page load times and technical issues
- **Content optimization:** Understands which content resonates with our audience

**Functional Cookies:** These cookies enhance your browsing experience:

- **Theme preferences:** Remembers your dark/light mode choice
- **Newsletter preferences:** Saves your subscription settings
- **Language preferences:** Maintains your preferred language settings
- **Reading progress:** Tracks your position in long articles

## Types of Cookies We Use

**First-Party Cookies:** Set directly by TinkByte for website functionality and analytics.

**Third-Party Cookies:** Set by our trusted partners for analytics and content delivery:

- **Google Analytics:** Website analytics and performance monitoring
- **Cloudflare:** Content delivery and security services
- **Social media platforms:** When you interact with embedded content

## Cookie Duration

**Session Cookies:** Deleted when you close your browser

**Persistent Cookies:** Remain on your device for a specified period:

- **Analytics cookies:** Up to 26 months
- **Preference cookies:** Up to 12 months
- **Functional cookies:** Up to 6 months

## Managing Your Cookie Preferences

**Browser Settings:** You can control cookies through your browser settings:

- **Chrome:** Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies and other site data
- **Firefox:** Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
- **Safari:** Preferences > Privacy > Cookies and website data
- **Edge:** Settings > Cookies and site permissions

**Cookie Consent:** When you first visit our website, you'll see a cookie consent banner where you can:

- Accept all cookies
- Reject non-essential cookies
- Customize your preferences
- Learn more about each cookie type

## Impact of Disabling Cookies

**Essential Cookies:** Disabling these may affect website functionality and user experience.

**Analytics Cookies:** Disabling these won't affect your browsing experience but helps us improve our content and services.

**Functional Cookies:** Disabling these may require you to reset preferences on each visit.

## Newsletter and Email Tracking

When you subscribe to our newsletters (TinkByte Weekly, Build Sheet, Stackdown, Signal Drop, System Signal, and our monthly publications), we may use tracking technologies to:

- Monitor email delivery and open rates
- Track link clicks and engagement
- Personalize content based on your interests
- Improve our newsletter content and timing

You can opt out of email tracking by adjusting your email client settings or unsubscribing from our newsletters.

## Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect cookie data:

- **Secure transmission:** All cookies are transmitted over HTTPS
- **Limited access:** Only authorized personnel can access cookie data
- **Regular audits:** We regularly review our cookie practices
- **Data minimization:** We only collect necessary cookie data

## Third-Party Services

**Google Analytics:** We use Google Analytics with IP anonymization to protect your privacy. You can opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

**Cloudflare:** Provides content delivery and security services. Their cookies are essential for website performance and security.

**Social Media:** When you interact with embedded social media content, those platforms may set their own cookies according to their privacy policies.

## International Data Transfers

Some of our cookie data may be processed by service providers located outside your country. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international data transfers.

## Your Rights

**Access:** You can request information about the cookies we use and the data they collect.

**Deletion:** You can delete cookies through your browser settings or request deletion of specific data.

**Objection:** You can object to the use of certain cookies, particularly for analytics and marketing purposes.

**Portability:** You can request a copy of your cookie preferences and data.

## Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

- Posting the updated policy on our website
- Updating the "last updated" date
- Sending notification to newsletter subscribers for significant changes
